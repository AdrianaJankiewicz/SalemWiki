High Magic

High Magic is type of magic in which the practitioner uses rituals and specific spoken spells as a means to obtais supernatural abilities. It is a highly complex tradition where main purpose is to communicate with the Divine, whether that is in the form of deity or another spiritual being. Witches and Shamans are known to use this type of magic to interact with the lord of their religion, but many High Priest are known to practice High Magic to attempt to interact with their biblical God.

Types
Alchemy
Not much is known about this kind of Magic, but it falls under High Magic due to its complex wisdom of old masters and ceremonial magic practices.
Shamanism
The main purpose of this kind of magic is to honor the design of nature and to maintain stability in the world.
Maleficium
Maleficium, or Malice is a Latin term to describe Magic that is harmful, dangerous or malevolent. The term applies to any magical act that is intended to cause harm or death.
℘
Low Magic

More than not, this type of magic is used for beneficial purposes, such as protection and healing. Instead of complex rituals, it used the power hidden in colours, herbs and images, as well alchemy, astrology and hypnosis. Low Magic is less controversial and can be practiced by anyone willing to learn.

Types
Contagious Magic
Contagious Magic is based on the principle belief that contact with or proximity to a certain object, person or place can influence the other.
Cunningcraft
Used by adherents cakked "Cunning Ones". This type of magic is mainly used for divination ad low flow spells. Cunning Ones don't come with as many supernatural abilities so instead they resort to preparation of concoctions and potions.
Sympathetic Magic
It is based on the principle that "like attracts like" or "like produces like". It focuses on the beleve that whatever befalls a representational image or object of a person, location or item, will befall the target of the spell.
