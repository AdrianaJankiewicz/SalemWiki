Summary

John Alden returns to his home city Salem, Massochusetts after fighting the French and the Indians for seven years, only to find that his home is under a witch-panic and Mary Sibley, love of his life, is now married to his biggest enemy, the man who sent him to die - George Sibley.

Summary

Its ultimate goal is to head humanity to destroy itself. There is only one person on the earth who can stop this crazy plan, the very witch who birthed the devil, his mother Mary Sibley.
