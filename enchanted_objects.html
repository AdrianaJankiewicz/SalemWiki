 Malum
℘
USE: To prepare land for the Grand Rite and to spread the Witch Pox. Triggers violent visions when held.
℘
About

The Malum is an ancient realic and very powerful weapon used by witches. Essential object to start Grand Rite, it is a bronze apple-shaped relic. A tangled snake surrounded by flames is engraved on the apple. It can be open up to reveal a crystal quartz medallion with an internal flap of meat infected with the plague.
The Malum is protected by richly decorated box, engraved with various symbols related to witchcraft - a pentacle, snakes, a demon and a dragon bitting on its own tail.
Once opened, a plague spread to infect all those opposing Witches. Bodies of the infected cover in festering ulcers that secrete a foul-smelling liquid and cause extreme pain. The secretion is a corrosive hellish tar that corrodes whatever it touches.

 Traveler's Mask
℘
USE: To transport the owner wherever he wants.
℘
About

Belongs to Magistrate Hale and was probably acquired during his many travels. It is a mask that looks like a male face with slits for eyes and beard on pointed chin. It has two straps that allow the owner to tie it to their face. It allows one person to travel in one direction, the wearer has to find other means to travel back as the mask stays in the starting location.

 Angel's Tear
℘
USE: To cause massive destruction.
℘
About

Angel's Tear is a bright scarlet gemstone globe contained in a locket. Also known as Red Mercury. It is made from tears shed by Angels during their mission. The full potential and the composition that guards its scarlet color are unknown, but as mentioned by The Sentinel, it is a weapon of mass destruction. It is used with an Instrument that works like a time bomb.
