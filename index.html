What is Salem?


Salem is supernatural horror and historical fiction created by Adam Simon and Brannon Braga. The series, being the network's first original-scripted show, premiered on WGN America on April 20, 2014, and it became network's highest-rated series.
"Salem" is set in 17th century Massachusetts and explores town's infamous witch trials but also dares to show the dark and supernatural truth behind this period in American History. In Salem witches are real, but they are not what they seem.
