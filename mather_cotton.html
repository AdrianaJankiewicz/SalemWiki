℘
ABOUT:

Cotton Mather is one of the main male characters of Salem. He is a well-educated aristocrat who worsens Salem's witch hunt. He is an expert on witches and malice, he is also Puritan Reverend, son of the witch hunter Increase Mather. Cotton is a man of contradiction. He is a big believer in God and yet he has his own demons that make him too afraid to exorcise.
Not much is know about his past, but from what John Alden said, Cotton wore "feminine" clothing when he was a child. While his friends went to war or built a career, Cotton was sent to study theology at Harvard University.

℘
TRIVIA:
  • Cotton's description on the main WGN America website says: "Well educated aristocrat and former reverend, Cotton Mather finds himself married to a witch and is trying to find a way to escape, which is hard to do with Brown Jenkins eating his insides every time he does something wrong."

  • Cotton Mather is based on the real Cotton Mather who influenced New England both socially and politically. He was a Puritan minister, prolific author and pamphleteer.

  • The description given on Official Instagram with the promotional photos of the third season, reads as follows: "Cotton Mather: the reverend of a people destined for Hell."
